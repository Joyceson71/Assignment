About Us

Forging the Engineers of Tomorrow

The Computer Science and Engineering Department at Kings Engineering College is dedicated to delivering world-class technical education rooted in innovation, integrity and industry relevance.

Our curriculum blends rigorous theory with hands-on laboratory practice, collaborative projects and live industry internships — equipping graduates to lead in a rapidly evolving technology landscape.
